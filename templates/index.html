How to get your captcha token One-time per song

🔗 Open musichero.ai
  1. Click the link above — musichero.ai opens in a new tab.
  2. On the page, solve the hCaptcha (click the checkbox; complete any image challenge).
  3. As soon as it is solved, press F12 and open the Console tab.
  4. Paste the command below and press Enter:
document.querySelector('[name="h-captcha-response"]').value
  1. The console prints a long token starting with P0_eyJ…
  2. Copy it and paste it in the field below.
